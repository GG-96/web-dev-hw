Assignment 5

Shepards Pie

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Beef
  • Peas
  • Potatos
  • One Onion

Directions

  1. Peel potatos and boil until soft
  2. Cook onion and beef until the beef is browned
  3. Add beef, onion, corn, peas, into a baking dish covering the top with mashed potatos
  4. Bake at 400 for 20 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
